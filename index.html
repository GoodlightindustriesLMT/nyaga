Our Kenyan Candle Story

Crafting light and fragrance for Kenyan homes, one candle at a time.

Goodlight Industries was founded with a passion for creating exceptional candles that elevate everyday moments. We believe in the power of scent and light to transform a space, soothe the soul, and evoke beautiful memories.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to produce high-quality, handcrafted candles using only the finest sustainable materials. From sourcing premium waxes and lead-free wicks to blending unique, captivating fragrances, every step of our process is guided by a commitment to excellence and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the leading handcrafted candle brand in Kenya, known for our unwavering commitment to quality, innovative fragrances, and sustainable practices, bringing warmth and light to every home and fostering pride in local craftsmanship.

Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

Find quick and helpful answers to the most common questions about Goodlight candles and their care.

We primarily use natural soy wax, known for its clean, long-lasting burn and excellent scent throw. Some specialized candles may use a blend of soy and other natural waxes, always prioritizing natural and sustainable options for the best quality and environmental impact.

Our standard 8 oz (approx. 226g) candles typically have a burn time of 40-50 hours when properly cared for (e.g., wick trimmed, proper burn cycles). Larger candles in our collection can burn for up to 75 hours, providing extended enjoyment.

While our candles are crafted with natural, high-quality, phthalate-free ingredients, we always recommend exercising caution. Always keep burning candles out of reach of children and pets. Ensure adequate ventilation in the room, and most importantly, never leave a burning candle unattended. Safety should always be your top priority when enjoying candles.

Our commitment to being eco-friendly includes using natural soy wax (a renewable resource), lead-free cotton wicks, **phthalate-free fragrance oils, and striving for **recyclable or reusable packaging. We also embrace **small-batch production to minimize waste.

For an optimal burn, always trim your wick to 1/4 inch (6mm) before each use. On the first burn, allow the wax to melt across the entire surface of the candle to prevent tunneling. Burn for 2-4 hours at a time, and never leave unattended.
